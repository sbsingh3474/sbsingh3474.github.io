Welcome to Human Trafficking Awareness in Ontario

What is Human Trafficking?

Human trafficking is a serious crime where people are forced, tricked, or threatened into work or activities like sexual exploitation or unpaid labor. Ontario has the most cases of trafficking in Canada, with about 66% happening within the province. This shows how urgent it is to raise awareness and take action.
