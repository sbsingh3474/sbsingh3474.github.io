Technology and Human Trafficking

Technology can help and hurt in the fight against trafficking. Traffickers use social media and websites to find, trick, or control victims. On the other hand, police and organizations use technology like data analysis to catch traffickers and help victims. So while technology helps spread awareness, it also makes it easier for traffickers to hide.
